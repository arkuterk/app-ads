1. Introduction Welcome to Toy Magic. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile game. Our game is designed for a general audience, including children under the age of 13. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all our users, especially young children. 2. Children’s Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K Compliance) Toy Magic is a family-friendly game intended for users aged 0-12. No Personal Data Collection: We do not knowingly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) from children. Parental Consent: Since we do not collect personal data, we do not require verified parental consent for the core gameplay experience. 3. Voice Command Functionality Toy Magic features innovative voice-controlled mechanics. Microphone Usage: Microphone access is used exclusively for real-time processing of in-game voice commands (e.g., "Repair", "Magic"). No Recording: Voice data is processed locally and instantly on the device. We do not record, store, or transmit any audio or voice data to our servers or any third parties. 4. Advertising (AdMob and Google Ads) Toy Magic includes third-party advertisements to keep the game free to play. Family-Friendly Ads: We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. We have configured our ad settings to request "Families" compatible ads. Filtering: We strictly filter out sensitive categories. Only ads rated for "G" (General) or "PG" (Parental Guidance) are displayed to ensure they are appropriate for children aged 0-12. Identifiers: Third-party ad providers may use non-personal identifiers (such as Advertising ID) to provide frequency capping and basic analytics, in compliance with Google Play’s Families Policy. 5. Analytics We may use tools like Google Analytics for Firebase to understand game performance and stability (e.g., crash reports). This data is anonymous and does not include any personal information that could identify a child. 6. Permissions To provide the best experience, Toy Magic may request the following: Microphone: For voice command interactions. Internet Access: To load advertisements and sync basic game progress (non-personal). 7. Global Compliance We respect privacy laws worldwide, including: COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) - USA. GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) - EU. CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) - USA. 8. Contact Us If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at: arkuterk@gmail.com
