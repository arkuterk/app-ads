Toy Magic Privacy Policy 1. Introduction Welcome to Toy Magic. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our players, especially children aged 0-12. This game is designed to be a safe, fun, and educational environment. 2. Children’s Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K) We strictly follow COPPA and GDPR-K. We do NOT collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, addresses, or emails. 3. Child-Friendly Audio Guidance Toy Magic is designed with young children in mind who may not yet be able to read. Voice Instructions: The game uses pre-recorded audio cues to guide the child (e.g., "Clean," "Repair"). Privacy First: The app DOES NOT use the microphone. It does not record, listen to, or process any user voices or environmental sounds. 4. Advertising (Family-Safe) We use Google AdMob to keep the game free. Strict Filtering: All ads are filtered to a "G" (General) rating. Compliance: We use non-personalized ad settings to ensure maximum safety for our young audience. 5. Data Storage & Deletion Game progress is stored locally on your device. We do not store any player data on our servers. Since no personal data is collected, there is no personal data to delete. 6. Permissions Internet: Used solely to load family-friendly advertisements. No Sensitive Access: This app does not require access to your microphone, camera, or contacts. 7. Global Compliance We adhere to international standards, including GDPR (EU), CCPA (USA), and Google Play’s Families Policy. 8. Contact Us For any privacy-related questions: arkuterk@gmail.com 8.
